Who Are We?

At Activity Map, we are passionate about connecting people with exciting local activities. Founded in Bristol, our mission is to make it easier for residents and visitors alike to discover, join, and enjoy a variety of activities happening around the city.

From sports and boardgames to cultural events and social gatherings, Activity Map is your go-to platform for exploring and engaging with your community.

Our Mission

To make discovering and participating in local activities effortless and enjoyable for everyone.

Our Values

  • Community: Bringing people together to create vibrant and active communities.
  • Accessibility: Making it easy for anyone to find and join activities.
  • Innovation: Continuously improving to provide the best user experience.
  • Trust: Ensuring safety and reliability for confident participation.
